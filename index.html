About Me

Hi there! My name is Emma Baier, and I am a third year student studying Design and Cognitive Science at the University of California, Davis. Although I started my collegiate career as a Mechanical Engineering student, I find that the combination of art, design, technology, psychology, coding, linguistics, and philosophy is something I'm constantly inspired and intruiged by; it's a course of study I'm truly growing to love. It's a great addition to other things on the list of things I love, which includes dogs, puns, music, books, and the focus of this class, the internet.

This webpage acts as an archive of everything I've done during this wonderful web design studio class, Design 117, during this Fall Quarter 2018. Look below to see some pretty interesting content from class, as well as some helpful links, and some design inspiration. Thank you!
